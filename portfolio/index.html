About Me

I have an enthusiastic, visionary personality, and am always looking for ways to do more with less. I am an excellent learner, and always eager to pick up a new skill. I am familier with a variety of audio, video, and computer technologies, and what I don't know, I can usually pick up very fast. Above all, I am a Christian, saved by the grace of God, and I want every project I work on to bring glory and honor to Him.
